# 🍽️ BiteZ

> A modern, multi-platform food ordering & delivery management system for restaurants.

## Overview

BiteZ is a complete food ordering ecosystem designed for restaurant businesses. It includes:

- 🖥️ Admin dashboard for operations, reporting & staff management
- 🌐 Customer web app for seamless ordering & real-time tracking
- 📱 Mobile apps for customers & delivery partners (coming soon)
- 🧾 POS terminal, Kitchen Display System (KDS), and Table QR ordering

Designed with **scalability**, **real-time updates**, **multi-branch support**, and a **delightful developer experience** in mind. Built to grow with your business — from single-location cafes to multi-branch restaurant chains.
